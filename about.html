Our Story

Rooted in Resilience & Entrepreneurship

The story of JUST KEEPER is rooted in resilience, entrepreneurship, and years of real-world business experience.

Founder Vikash Goyal began his journey in the garment business at a very young age, after the sudden loss of his father. With family responsibilities and a deep connection to the industry, he stepped into business early — gradually building a strong understanding of fabrics, manufacturing, and the apparel market
